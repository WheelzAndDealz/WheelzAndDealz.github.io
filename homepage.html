About us

Wheelz n Dealz is based on the parkmore road in Ballybrit. Our dealer has been around since 1985 where we've been offering great cars at a great price. We have a wide range of fine automobiles from brands like Toyota, VW, Audi and Seat. If you or someone you love is looking for a new car, feel free to contact us on our contact page where you can fill in an inquiry, find out our opening hours and information about our student deals. If you're currently not looking for a car at the moment, don't be shy to come and check out our Wheelz n Dealz shop where we can give you the supplies and products to keep your car looking fantastic every single day.
